Chickens in your Yard

If you like eggs, and pets, you might like to have a chicken coop in your backyard.

Not all chickens are alike, some are tame and can become very friendly to you and your family, some lay lots of eggs, some lay very few eggs. You want a breed that is fairly low maintenance if you're looking to have a coop in your yard.

These eggs look great don't they? If you want to raise chickens in your back yard, even in a city yard, be sure to pick the right kind of chicken for you and your family. Not all chickens are alike! Here is a nice place to start learning about your backyard chickens!
